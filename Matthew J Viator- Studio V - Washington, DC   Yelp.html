      History

      Established in 2009.

      *There are other reviews not shown: please follow the gray shade link to read a full listing of reviews.* The Studio was established to dynamically engage students in a love of musical education.  The Studio constantly seeks out new educational methods and opportunities in this quest.  Students from all walks of life, races, ages, orientations, and perspectives come together with a common goal: the enrichment of their lives through musical education and performance.  Grounded in weekly lessons with Mr. Viator, the studio has constantly sought to add educational value to the lives of its students.  In this effort, the studio has come to provide monthly student socials, recitals, private concerts and lectures, field trips, and other such supplements as able.  Today, the Studio consistently ranks as one of the top private musical studios in the DC Metro Area, seeking to continue building its solid reputation of student-centric education and enrichment at the piano and in music, generally.

      Meet the Business Owner

      Matthew V. Business Owner

      Matthew J Viator is a composer, pianist, and vocal coach living in Washington, DC.  Mr. Viator is a graduate of the Peabody Conservatory of Music of the Johns Hopkins University, studying with Christopher Theofanidis, Nicholas Maw, Shafer Mahoney, Corey McVicor, David Smooke, and Libby Larson.  Mr. Viator has also studied with noted Pedagogue Gigi Reeks.

      Active since 2000 with works and performances in various venues across the nation, Mr. Viator's compositional style is influenced by minimalism, traditional jazz, modernism, and classical counterpoint.  Among his particular musical strengths, Mr. Viator is a skilled writer of Baroque Counterpoint, Russian Music and Analysis, and the Musicological study of the American Modernists, 1920 - 1950.

      Among the technical traditions used, Mr. Viator teaches students the schools of Clementi, Leschevitzky, with a constant eye towards efficacy and physical / emotional achievement. Mr. Viator is a sixth generation teacher in the Beethoven lineage.
